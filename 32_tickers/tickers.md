#### V implements the ticker pattern with a goroutine + channel loop. The tick timestamps will depend on when the example runs.
___
##### Run Command:

`$ v run tickers.v`

`Tick at 2009-11-10 23:00:00 +0000 UTC m=+0.500000001`
`Tick at 2009-11-10 23:00:00 +0000 UTC m=+1.000000001`
`Tick at 2009-11-10 23:00:00 +0000 UTC m=+1.500000001`
`Ticker stopped`
